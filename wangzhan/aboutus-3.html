Statue of Liberty: Anxiety on the Way Chaos

The Statue of Liberty, a symbol of freedom and hope, can sometimes evoke unexpected emotions. For me, it was anxiety. I had planned to visit Liberty Island, booking an 11 a.m. ferry ticket with excitement. But waking up late threw everything into chaos.Rushing through the crowded subway, heart pounding, I could only pray I wouldn’t miss the ferry. Every delayed stop, e very crowded platform amplified my worries. By the time I arrived, the tension had peaked—but the sight of the towering statue was both a relief and a reminder of resilience. Sometimes, even the path to freedom can feel suffocating.
